ChatPat Recipe

Description

In this recipe you will learn how to you make "ChatPat" at your home using your home ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Puffed Rice
  • Dalmot
  • Onion
  • Chilly
  • Salt
  • ChatPat Masala
  • Lemon
  • Peas
  • Peanut
  • Coriander
  • Potato
  • Tomato

Steps:

  1. Cut Onions,Coriander,Potato and Tomato into small pieces.
  2. Cursh the Noodle.
  3. Mix the Noodle, Puffed Rice, Onions, Coriander, Potato, Tomato, Peanut, Peas and Dalmot in a container.
  4. Put some ChatPat Masala and mix it again.
  5. Put some salt and lemon as per your taste and mix it well.
  6. Finally, your ChatPat is ready, serve it on a plate and enjoy!
