Daal Recipe

Description

In this recipe you will learn how to make "Daal" at your home.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Water
  • Tumeric
  • Daal
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

Steps:

  1. Use a 1/2 cup of Daal.
  2. Rinse it well in a pressure cooker.
  3. Add two cup of water.
  4. Add a table spoon of salt and a tea spoon of turmeric.
  5. Close the lid of the pressure cooker and cook for 3 to 4 whistle in medium flame.
  6. After it is cooked, let it cool down for 5 mins.
  7. Heat oil and fry some chopped onion, garlic and ginger until it is golden brown.
  8. Now transfer your fried item into the cooked daal.
  9. Stir it well.
  10. Now, you daal is ready!
